Best Skin Infection Treatment in Pondicherry

Skin infections can be caused by a wide variety of germs, and symptoms can vary from mild to serious. These can include bacterial skin infections, as well as viral, fungal, or parasitic ones. Your skin is the largest organ of your body. Its function is to protect your body from infection. Sometimes the skin itself becomes infected.

How is a skin infection diagnosed? üëÅÔ∏è

A good medical exam is the best way to determine what is causing a skin infection. Often, doctors can identify the type of skin infection based on the appearance and location. Your doctor may ask about your symptoms and closely examine any bumps, rashes, or lesions. For example, ringworm often causes a distinct circular, scaly rash. In other cases, a sample of skin cells can help your doctor determine the type of infection.

How is a skin infection treated?üëÅÔ∏è

Treatment depends on the cause of the infection and the severity. Some types of viral skin infections may improve on their own within days or weeks. Bacterial infections are often treated with topical antibiotics applied directly to the skin or with oral antibiotics. If the strain of bacteria is resistant to treatment, treating the infection may require intravenous antibiotics administered in the hospital.

Benefits

You can use over-the-counter antifungal sprays and creams to treat a fungal skin infection. If your condition doesn't improve, ask your doctor about prescription oral or topical creams. In addition, you can apply medicated creams to your skin to treat parasitic skin infections. Your doctor may also recommend medications to reduce discomfort like anti-inflammatory drugs.
