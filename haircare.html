Understanding Hair Loss

Managing hair loss is something a lot of us struggle with. With no dearth of information out in the world, we try all sorts of home remedies from extreme diets to various medications and shampoos. All these leave us more puzzled than when we originally began. Opting for hair loss treatment from the specialized hair clinic helps you cut through da the noise. Also it promotes hair regrowth in the best possible way.

Causes Of Hair Loss

Hair loss occurs due to various reasons, it depends on individual factors, environmental, dietary and much more. A few of the predominant causes of hair fall are: Family history (heredity) The most common cause of hair loss is a hereditary condition that happens with aging. This condition is called androgenic alopecia, male-pattern baldness and female-pattern baldness.

Medications and supplements

Hair loss can be a side effect of certain drugs, such as those used for cancer, arthritis, depression, heart problems, gout and high blood pressure. Radiation therapy to the head The hair may not grow back the same as it was before.

A very stressful event

Many people experience a general thinning of hair several months after a physical or emotional shock. This type of hair loss is temporary.

Hairstyles and treatments

Excessive hairstyling or hairstyles that pull your hair tight, such as pigtails or cornrows, can cause a type of hair loss called traction alopecia. Hot-oil hair treatments and permanents also can cause hair to fall out. If scarring occurs, hair loss could be permanent.
